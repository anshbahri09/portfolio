Skateboarding - craze to passion

October 1, 2013 Ansh Bahri

Skateboarding is one of the most adventurous and daring hobbies in the history of sports. It’s amazing to witness twists and turns in the air. You can experience the best of aerial tricks through skateboarding. You have to overpower the fear of injuries to master the art of skateboarding. Besides mastering the relevant skills, immense resilience and courage is what is required to excel in this sport. Skateboarders glide into the air and perform stunts on benches, rails, stairs, elevated platforms and roads. Skateboarding becomes a passion for many as this sport lets you make all your fantasies true. A lot of practice, perseverance and passion are what is needed to become a good skateboarder.


To understand why skateboarding is a passion today, we have to peep into the history of skateboarding. There was a time when bicycles were very expensive. Children used wooden rafts to sail over the water and slide over ice during winters. During those times, skates were the most suitable alternatives to bicycles. They were cheap, very poorly designed, and could be broken very easily. Then the children started using them with a handle to carry groceries so that shopping became more convenient for them. This was how skateboard was used in the initial days. The invention of these skateboards was basically out of a need to find out a cheap alternative to the expensive bicycles, which could be portable and fast. Gradually, owning to its attractive and fashionable get up, skateboarding craze became the norm. Now-a days, it has become a passion in the young generation, and teenagers often go crazy about this sport and their skateboards.

Electric skateboards

With the advent of electric skateboards in the world market, skateboarding craze has taken a completely new dimension. These skateboards allow their users to have immense controlling power on the board. Sharp turns, excellent brakes and high durability are some outstanding features of electric skateboards that made it climb up on the charts of popularity as soon as they were introduced in the global market. Constant innovation and redesigning has been undertaken by leading skateboard manufacturers to keep the increasing demand for skateboarding soaring high.

Stunts

A lot of time and patience, not to mention any injuries as well, needs to be invested to learn how to perform stunts on skateboards. It’s advisable to get trained under the watchful eyes of experienced professionals to limit the risks involved and learn the tricks of the sport quickly. Stunts that can be performed on skateboards have also taken the craze for skateboards to a new level.
